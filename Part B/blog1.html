Boston Marathon

The Boston Marathon is an annual marathon race hosted by several cities and towns in greater Boston in eastern Massachusetts, United States. It is traditionally held on Patriots' Day, the third Monday of April.

The Boston Marathon will be held on 17th April 2023. It will begin from Main Street in the rural New England town of Hopkinton and will finish at Boston Public Library in Copley Square.
